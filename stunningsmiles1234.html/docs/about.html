Dr. Birdevinder Attri

Dev Attri, DDS brings to you his 20 years of experience as a Dentist. Dr. Attri grew up in India and graduated from Government Dental College, Patiala in 1998. After graduation, he ran a successful Dental practice in his hometown before ambition and desire for better life drew him and his loving wife to America. He passed his qualifying exams and was accepted to the prestigious UCSF School of Dentistry from where he graduated in 2011. Since then, he has worked as an associate dentist in a corporate setting as well as for various small and large dental practices in Northern California before calling Stunning Smiles, Santa Rosa his home. Dr. Attri is licensed in California and affiliated with the Holistic Dental Association. As your family dentist, he endeavors to continue a holistic approach to dental care with an emphasis on mercury-free and latex-free dentistry. He believes in customizing dental treatment care plans to your unique situation and welcomes your family’s participation in developing a collaborative treatment plan. Dr. Attri very well understands the fear surrounding dental visits experienced by some. His steady-handed and gentle approach coupled with a relaxed, homely surrounding environment and our wonderful friendly staff will surely calm your fears and anxieties. Besides Dentistry, Dev Attri, enjoys eating out on lazy days at San Francisco with his wife and two sons. He de-compresses himself by meeting old friends, watching Seinfeld, reading books, hiking and meditating.
